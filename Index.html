About Me

Venerable Middeniye Dhammasena Thero

Blessed with the opportunity to walk the noble path of the Buddha's teachings, I am Middeniye Dhammasena Thero. As a Buddhist monk and dedicated student, I strive to embody the principles of mindfulness, compassion, and wisdom in every aspect of life.

Beyond my spiritual practice, I have cultivated a deep passion for digital creativity. Through web design, photography, and graphic design, I find unique ways to share the beauty of Dhamma and the natural world — bridging ancient wisdom with modern expression.
